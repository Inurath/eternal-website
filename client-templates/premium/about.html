Our Story

Born from a belief that genuine wellness should be accessible, personal, and profoundly effective - not just expensive.

Founded in 2018

Built Around You,
Not Around Protocols

Dr. Sofia Marchetti founded Elara after 12 years working in clinical settings where she watched patients get treated as conditions, not people. She wanted to build something different.

Elara was designed from the ground up to be the kind of place where every practitioner knows your name, your history, and your goals - and every treatment is genuinely tailored to you.

Today we have a team of 8 licensed specialists and have served over 3,000 clients across Sarasota County. Our approach combines evidence-based treatments with a luxury, unhurried environment that makes every visit feel restorative from the moment you walk in.

What We Believe

Our Philosophy

Every decision we make at Elara comes back to these four principles.

Whole-Person Care

We treat the whole person, not isolated symptoms. Body, mind, and spirit are connected - our programs reflect that.

Evidence-Based

Every treatment we offer is grounded in clinical research. We don't offer wellness theater - we offer results.

True Luxury

Luxury isn't about price - it's about the quality of attention. Every client receives our full focus and care.

Long-Term Partnership

We're not in the business of one-off treatments. We want to be your wellness partner for years - and we build that relationship intentionally.
