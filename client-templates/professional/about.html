About Summit Property Group

Family-owned, locally rooted, and built on the belief that buying or selling a home should be straightforward and stress-free.

Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Results

Summit Property Group was founded in 2010 by Carlos Rivera after 10 years working at larger firms. His belief was simple: clients deserve an agent who treats their transaction like it's the most important deal in the world - because for them, it is.

Today, Summit is a team of dedicated professionals covering all aspects of Sarasota County real estate. We've closed over 850 transactions totaling more than $280 million in sales volume, but we measure our success by the relationships we build and the referrals we earn.

We're not a franchise. We don't have quotas to hit. Every client gets our full attention and a strategy tailored specifically to their situation.
